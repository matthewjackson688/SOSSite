Introducing #SOS, replacing first paragraph
The story starts with Disability in Wales and Africa (DWA), a very small international disabled persons organisation (DPO) with a goal of global solidarity within the international disabled community. The DWA had the same goal and a project known as Tell Your Story but wanted to develop a more inclusive approach so Share Our Story was born. #SOS became its own organisation.
