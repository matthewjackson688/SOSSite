Get Involved and Support Us

Have these stories resonated with you? #SOS is about understanding the direct experience of disability in Africa and Wales. There are several ways you can get involved in and support #SOS.

  1. If you are a disabled person or an organisation run by disabled people, we invite you to join the #SOS WhatsApp chat. Click here.
  2. Whoever you are, please find us on our current social media outlets: Facebook, LinkedIn, and X. Follow and like to keep up to date.
  3. If you are an organisation, we invite you to share your logo on our webpage. Click here.
  4. Get in touch to see how your company/organisation/service could remove barriers and become more disability inclusive. Click here.
  5. If you would like to donate funds, materials, or in-kind support, please click here.
